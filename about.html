School History

The Exalter Minaret Schools is a faith based School that was established on the September 2015 at Ajefemi street ketu Lagos with eight pupils and five teachers, having five rooms for creche pupils and nursery 1 and 2 in 2017, the school expanded to include Higher Grader.

In 2018, the school was divided into Nursery, Lower, Higher Grades and now College. The school has won severally both academically and in sporting activities
